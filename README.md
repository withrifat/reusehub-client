# reusehub-client
# 🌍 ReuseHub — Give Life to Your Unused Items

![ReuseHub Banner](assets/logo.png)

## 📌 Overview

**ReuseHub** is a modern, community-driven platform designed to turn unused items into valuable resources for others. Instead of letting items sit idle or go to waste, users can share, exchange, or give them away to people who actually need them.

This platform focuses on **sustainability**, **community support**, and **responsible consumption**.

---

## 🚀 Features

- ♻️ **Item Reuse System**  
  Share unused items like books, clothes, electronics, and furniture.

- 🤝 **Community Exchange**  
  Connect with people who need what you no longer use.

- 🆓 **Free or Low-Cost Sharing**  
  Items can be given away or exchanged at minimal cost.

- 📍 **Location-Based Discovery**  
  Find items available near your area.

- ❤️ **Save & Track Items**  
  Bookmark items and track availability status.

- 🔍 **Smart Filtering**  
  Filter items by category, location, or type (Free / Exchange).

---

## 🎯 Purpose

### Why ReuseHub?

- **Reduce Waste**  
  Minimize unnecessary disposal of usable items.

- **Support People**  
  Help those who can’t afford essential products.

- **Build Awareness**  
  Encourage a sustainable and mindful lifestyle.

- **Database:** MongoDB
